About Me

Not just a
designer.
A brand's
secret weapon.

I'm Austin Nwaigwe. For nine years, I've been the person brands call when they're tired of looking average. I've worked across technology, finance, real estate, and entertainment — and in every industry, the problem is always the same: a brand that doesn't do the business justice.

As Head of Design at 4TS Technologies, I built the visual identity for a phone manufacturer from the ground up — brand system, product launch collateral, the works. At MIM Finance, I translated complex financial services into a brand that commanded trust on sight.

I don't decorate. I solve. Every colour, typeface, and layout decision I make has a reason — and that reason is always to make your brand undeniable.
